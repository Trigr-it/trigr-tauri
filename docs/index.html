How it works

From zero to working
hotkey in 60 seconds.

No config files. No syntax to learn. If you can click a key on a keyboard, you can automate it.

1

Pick your modifier

Click Ctrl, Alt, Shift or Win. The keyboard shows what's already assigned and what's free — at a glance, no guessing.

2

Click a key

Click any key. Pick an action — Type Text, Run Macro, Send Hotkey, Open App. Fill in the detail. That's it.

3

It fires immediately

Hit Assign. No restart. Your hotkey is live in every app right now. Change it any time in two clicks.
